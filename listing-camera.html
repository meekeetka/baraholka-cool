📍 Portland, OR  ·  ♻️ Used — excellent (9.5/10)

Description

Selling my Canon EOS 90D body. I bought it new in February 2022 and used it for street photography and occasional wildlife work. With only 4,800 shutter actuations (Canon rates the shutter at 100,000 cycles), this camera has a very long life ahead of it — it's essentially new in terms of wear.

The 32.5MP APS-C sensor produces excellent image quality with wide dynamic range. The 45-point all-cross-type AF system is fast and reliable, especially for moving subjects. The dual-pixel RAW AF works well in Live View. The optical viewfinder has 100% coverage — a real advantage for framing.

Comes with the original Canon box, LP-E6NH battery, LC-E6 charger, neck strap, body cap, and eyecup. No lens included. Happy to do a test shoot during meetup in inner SE Portland.
