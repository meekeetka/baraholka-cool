Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025. This page explains how Baraholka Cool collects and uses your data.

What we collect How we use it Cookies Third parties Your rights

1. What data we collect

Baraholka Cool collects the minimum data necessary to operate the platform.

Data you provide voluntarily:

  • Name or username when registering or posting a listing;
  • Contact information: phone number and/or email, if you include them in your listing or contact form;
  • Listing content: photos, titles, descriptions, prices, location;
  • Messages you send through the contact form or between users;
  • Ratings and reviews if you leave them.

Data we collect automatically:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (city/region);
  • Browser type and version, operating system;
  • Pages you visit, time spent on the site;
  • Date and time of your visit;
  • Referral source (how you arrived at the site).

We do not collect payment data (card numbers, banking details) — all financial transactions happen directly between buyer and seller.

2. How we use your data

Collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and maintaining platform functionality (posting, searching, and viewing listings);
  • Facilitating contact between buyers and sellers;
  • Content moderation and fraud prevention;
  • Technical analysis and improving the site;
  • Responding to your requests and inquiries;
  • Complying with applicable law.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing without your explicit consent.

3. Cookies and analytics

Our site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary cookies — ensure basic site functionality (session data, language settings). Do not require consent.
  • Analytics cookies — we use Google Analytics 4 for anonymous traffic and behavior analysis. Data is sent to Google and processed under their Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising cookies — if advertising is displayed via Google AdSense, Google may collect data for personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note: disabling necessary cookies may affect site functionality.

4. Third parties

We may share data with the following categories of third parties:

  • Hosting and cloud service providers — to store data and keep the site running;
  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analytics;
  • Google AdSense — displaying ads, if you haven't opted out of personalized advertising;
  • Law enforcement — only on the basis of lawful requests under applicable law.

5. Your rights

Under applicable law and the GDPR you have the right to:

  • obtain information about what data we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate data;
  • request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten");
  • object to certain types of data processing;
  • withdraw previously given consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@baraholka-cool.net.
